Auto Music Redstone System

Download Schematic

Requirements & Installation

  • Glass can be replaced with any block.
  • Stone can be replaced with any block.
  • Trapdoor can be replaced with any trapdoor or block, or may be omitted.

Schematic Installation

After downloading the file, follow the steps

  • WorldEdit: gamefolder/config/worldedit/schematics/
  • Litematica: gamefolder/schematics/

Game Folder Locations

Linux:
~/.minecraft
Windows:
C:\Users\<YourUserName>\AppData\Roaming\.minecraft
macOS:
/Users/<YourUserName>/Library/Application Support/minecraft

WorldEdit Instructions

//schematic load automusicredstonesystem.schem
//paste

Litematica Instructions

  1. First, since it starts from the schematic ground, break the block you are on, that is, go down one block.
  2. Press the default M key to open the Litematica menu.
  3. Select Load Schematics from the menu:
  4. Select your schematic file:
  5. Click Load Schematic to place it:

Gereksinimler ve Kurulum

  • Cam yerine herhangi bir blok kullanılabilir.
  • Taş yerine herhangi bir blok kullanılabilir.
  • Tuzak kapısı herhangi bir tuzak kapısı veya blok olabilir veya opsiyonel.

Şematik Kurulumu

Dosyayı indirdikten sonra adımları takip edin
